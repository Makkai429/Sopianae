Today's Culture

Sopianae is not just an archaeological site —it is a living part of Pécs’s cultural identity. The city embraces its >Roman heritage through festivals, educational programs, and public art that celebrate the ancient past.

Festivals and Events

The annual Sopianae Reviviscit festival brings Roman history to life with:

  • Historical reenactments
  • Roman-style markets
  • Performances and workshops
  • Interactive activities for families

These events allow visitors to experience the atmosphere of a Roman provincial town.

Museums and Education

Local museums offer exhibitions on Roman history and early Christianity, while schools and universities incorporate Sopianae into their educational programs. Guided tours, lectures, and workshops help residents and visitors understand the significance of the site.

Urban Integration

Modern Pécs integrates ancient remains into its urban landscape. Glass walkways, open-air displays, and carefully preserved ruins allow the public to encounter Roman history in everyday spaces. This blending of past and present creates a unique cultural environment where history is not hidden —it is part of the city’s rhythm.
